Easy Insight offers consulting services to support product integration into anything from quick small business configurations to sophisticated enterprise engagements. Our consulting services focus around covering the full spectrum from the overall strategic business objectives to the underlying technical implementation. While delivering a quality installation that meets the need of every day technical users, we make sure that the delivery also meets the requirements of the higher level business goals.

Our business consulting team provides expertise in lean, six sigma, and other process improvement methodologies. Using proven best practices, we work with you to identify the pain points in your business, then break those problems down into more and more achievable sections of work.

Once those sections of work are identified, our technical consulting helps take you the rest of the way. With extensive experience in business intelligence, enterprise integration, and general software development, we're able to take the defined business goals and translate those into the necessary integrations with the rest of your infrastructure.
