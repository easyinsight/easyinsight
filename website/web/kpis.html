Key Performance Indicators

Easily define Key Performance Indicators (KPIs) to keep a pulse on those metrics that matter the most to your business. Build executive scorecards spanning your entire business or just measure the key metrics in your Basecamp or Highrise system!

KPIs

Start by defining Key Performance Indicators, those big metrics that best define your key business goals. Whether it's financial, sales, project management, software development tracking, or any other source of data, track the numbers that matter.

Scorecards

Take those KPIs and assemble them into executive scorecards. Combine metrics from different sources into a single coherent display. View the scorecard on Easy Insight, have it emailed to you on a regular basis, or look at it on your smartphone!

KPI Trees

Combine KPIs into a hierarchial view from the highest level business goals down into individual divisions across the organization, giving you a clear view of business status across as many as divisions of the company as you desire.
