Feature Overview

Easy Insight offers a wide range of functionality including an easy to use drag and drop report editor, instant connections to import data from a wide variety of SaaS systems and rich KPI capabilities.

Instant SaaS Reporting

Secure connections to a wide variety of external services enable you to see results in a couple of clicks. An extract tool helps to load relational data from MySQL, SQL Server, or Oracle into Easy Insight. Even upload a flat file or spreadsheet!

Easy Report Building

Easy Insight's report editor will have you building custom reports right out of the gate! A fully web based interface provides support for ad hoc creation of lists, charts, maps, gauges, and more. Dynamically create interactive filters to restrict the report to smaller data sets. Link between reports or to entirely different systems! Set up hierarchies to roll up or drill down into data. Create complex mathematical calculations or augment your data with custom fields.

Export the Reports

Once you have the perfect report you can export the results into Excel or setup scheduled email delivery to see your weekly billings or to-do assignments. Ensure users don't have to incorporate a new system into their day to day routine by embedding reports and dashboards into existing applications through embedded HTML.
