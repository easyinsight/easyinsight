Click and connect to a variety of SaaS systems

Simply sign up for Easy Insight on our website and you're up and running with no downloads or additional installs. In a matter of a couple of clicks, you can connect to a wide variety of sources ranging from Basecamp and Zendesk to Salesforce and Quickbase.

Once connected, data will automatically synchronize between systems to display an intricate picture of your business with no further effort required. Affordable and intuitive means Easy Insight is accessible to all, yet does not sacrifice quality. There's no need for development or integration consulting, extensive BI knowledge or background, or excessive funds to get the same data results and pixel-perfect visualizations offered by larger BI platforms.

Import your on premise data

Easy Insight offers multiple options to securely import data from MySQL, SQL Server, Oracle, and PostgreSQL. You can pull over entire tables, simple selects, or complex queries spanning multiple tables. Big data from Amazon Redshift can easily be integrated for broader reporting. Easy Insight can also import CSV or Excel files.

Develop your own data sources

Push your own data into Easy Insight through a JSON or XML API or point Easy Insight at pulling data from an arbitrary JSON endpoint. Either way, you'll get the same business intelligence capabilities.

Data warehousing made easy

There's no need to deal with the hassles of creating complicated builds for star schema and fact tables with Easy Insight. As you connect to your various data sources, Easy Insight bypasses these burdens and gets you directly connected to the reports and dashboards you need to understand your business metrics in a matter of a couple clicks.

Combine your data

Combine different data sources to enable reporting across your entire system. You can simply put reports from different systems together into a single dashboard or actually join the underlying data sources together to create calculations across different systems. Build a single view of the customer from lead generation through project management and invoicing or extend your SaaS systems with spreadsheet or database data.

Federated sources

Have multiple accounts of Basecamp, Salesforce, or other systems in your organization? Copies of the same database schema for different customers? You can federate these sources into Easy Insight for consolidated reporting and build reports against those systems which behave identically to those operating against a single system.

Drag. Drop. Done. Easy report creation

Using Easy Insight’s Drag, Drop, and Done report builder enables you to quickly and intuitively build a wide variety of reports in a matter of clicks. Easily create reports specific to your data needs to not only uncover enterprise opportunities, but also mishaps, lags, and threats.

Innotative and creative visualizations

Explore data through an array of highly visual, creative and interactive reports and charts. Choose from a variety of customizable charts that include traditional bar, pie and line graphs, to more advanced visualization options such as scatter plots and heat maps. Once you’ve found the right chart, tailor the display with custom styling to make it fit with your internal corporate branding and color scheme.

Slice and dice your way through data discovery

Do you need a way to flawlessly shift between data views, and navigate data to uncover data discover not easily recognizable in a one-dimensional chart? Easy Insight enables you to slice and dice data to navigate multi-dimensional drill paths through online analytic processing (OLAP). Drill into details from a single chart or combine several charts in a single dashboard to drill into multiple views of a single discovery.

Mobile reports and dashboards

Any Easy Insight report and dashboard automatically renders just as well on phones or tablets. With a caress and tap of the screen, drill-down, uncover, collaborate data anytime, anywhere with your mobile device. With Easy Insight, you are no longer chained to a desktop, but can analyze data and share discoveries on the go.

Distribute to your team

Schedule reports and dashboards to be emailed as an attachment or exported to a PDF, Excel, or CSV document. Embed your reports and dashboards into external sites so that your users have seamless access to their data without needing to use another system.

Ensure that the right people see the right data

Easy Insight securely enables you to restrict access to both specific reports and dashboards, as well as to set finely grained permissions on the actual data so that each user sees ony his or her own particular slice of data Through a series of permission labels, and pre-assigned roles, you can allow data access and interactions with that data to only those granted permission. As you configure these data level security permissions, they're automatically applied to any reports or dashboards the user views.
