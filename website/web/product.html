Feature Overview

Easy Insight offers you a wide range of functionality, ranging from an easy to use drag and drop report editor, instant connections to import data from a wide variety of SaaS systems and rich KPI capabilities.

Instant SaaS Reporting

Connections to a wide variety of external services enable you to see results in a couple of clicks. Using 37Signals Basecamp and Highrise? Salesforce? Google Analytics? FreshBooks? Easy Insight can securely connect to these external SaaS systems in a couple of clicks. Want reporting on your relational data in MySQL, SQL Server, or Oracle? An extract tool will help you load that data into Easy Insight. Just want to upload a flat file or spreadsheet? Easy enough!

Easy Report Building

Easy Insight's report editor will have you building custom reports right out of the gate! A fully web based interface provides support for the ad hoc creation of lists, charts, maps, gauges, and more. Dynamically create interactive filters to restrict the report to that smaller data set you want to see. Link between reports or to entirely different systems! Set up hierarchies to roll up or drill down into data. Create complex mathemetical calculations or augment your data with custom fields.

Export the Reports

Once you have a report giving you the answers you need, you can export the results. More comfortable with Excel? Export the report into Excel and manipulate the data from there. Need to see your weekly billings or daily todo assignments? Set up scheduled email delivery. Want to view the reports in another dashboard? Embed reports and scorecards into your iGoogle page, your Basecamp site, or any other location that can host embedded HTML.
