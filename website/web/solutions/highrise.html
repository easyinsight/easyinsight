Answers to Your Highrise Reporting Questions

Highrise can give you simple summaries on your sales pipeline, but in order to see the full picture you're going to need analytics on top of the CRM management. View sales pipelines by tags, or view contracts by dates. Get running totals for multiple sections of the pipeline, instead of just breakdowns by pipeline stage. Get breakdowns of which contracts will require on-site work / travel vs. those that can be done remotely.

Prebuilt Reports to Get You Started

Whether it's a pie chart showing your deal amounts by category, a tree giving you a hierarchical report on your prospects, or a simple list of contacts with a variety of optional filters, our rich library of prebuilt reports can help you get a quick start. Build a particularly cool report? You can add it to help others with a single click.
