Answers to Your Highrise Reporting Questions

Need to build Highrise reports on your companies, cases, deals, emails, tasks, contacts, or notes? Easy Insight gives you the ability to create simple lists of contact information, pie charts of deal dollars by company, line charts of contact creation over time, and anything else you may want! Ad hoc report creation allows you to drag in fields from any of the key Highrise subject matters to create the reports you need to manage and optimize your sales activities. You can apply dynamic filters to restrict the data down to a particular date range, show only deals matching a certain tag, or only open cases.

Extend Your Highrise Reporting With Custom Fields

Easy Insight automatically pulls over all of your custom fields on contacts and companies from Highrise so that you can add those new fields into your reports. Export, Email, and Embed

You can export any report into Excel, PDF, and PNG to share in meetings or fit into current business processes. You can set up reports to deliver over email on a scheduled basis so that you have a daily or weekly view of your hot leads. You can even embed reports into external dashboards so that they're visible to the rest of your organization. Extensive styling and formatting capabilities make sure that you can display reports in terms of corporate style standards, including logo images and other custom branding.
