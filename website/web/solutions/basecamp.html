Intro

This solution provides an integration of 37Signals Basecamp to Easy Insight, enabling sophisticated reporting and analysis of your Basecamp projects. The solution provides coverage of both Todo Items and Time Tracking.

Installation

Click the link to the left to be taken to the Easy Insight application. Once there, click on the button on the left to install the Basecamp solution into your account. After installing the data source, you'll be prompted for your Basecamp URL and credentials. Once you hit Create with a valid URL and credentials, Easy Insight will pull over key aggregate data from your Basecamp installation through the API. This process may take a little time, depending on the size of your installation.

Post Installation

After installation, a set of prebuilt reports will appear for you, providing a standard set of views into your Todos and Time Tracking data. You can adjust the filters in those reports, click Edit the Report to go in and make deeper changes, or navigate into the data source itself to create your own reports.
