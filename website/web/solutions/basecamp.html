Answers to Your Basecamp Reporting Questions

Easy Insight provides you with the tools to quickly perform Basecamp reporting. You can create custom lists and charts across your projects, milestones, todo lists, and time tracking hours. Need to look at data across subject matters, like time tracking hours over a hierarchy of Project, Milestone, and Todo List? No problem! Our ad hoc report editor enables you to easily drag and drop the fields you want into the reports you want, filter the results to refine the view, and export the data to Excel, iGoogle, and more. You can even set up recurring email delivery of reports to provide a daily or weekly update on project status to a wider team.
