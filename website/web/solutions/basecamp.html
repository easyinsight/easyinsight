Want the ability to perform custom Basecamp reporting? Answers to Your Basecamp Reporting Questions

Easy Insight provides you with the tools to quickly perform Basecamp reporting. Our ad hoc report editor enables you to easily drag and drop the fields you want into the reports you want, filter the results to refine the view, and export the data to Excel, iGoogle, and more.

Prebuilt Reports to Get You Started

Whether it's a burndown chart showing your status on the current milestone, a tree giving you a hierarchical report on your hours worked, or a simple list of todo items with a variety of optional filters, our rich library of prebuilt reports can help you get a quick start. Build a particularly cool report? You can add it to help others with a single click.

Extend Your Basecamp Reporting With Custom Fields

Want to add priority to your todo items? Billing rate to your time tracking? Easy Insight provides you with the functionality to easily extend your Basecamp reporting with custom fields. Have Highrise too? Combine your Basecamp and Highrise data to create a single dashboard of todos, hours, and deal activity by user across the two products.
