What's New With Easy Insight
Log into Easy Insight

Custom Fields on Highrise

We've rolled out new connections to three systems--Batchbook, a small business CRM service, Harvest, a small business invoicing and time tracking service, and Zendesk, one of the leading SaaS help desk services. All three of these connections require the Basic tier of service on Easy Insight. Enjoy!

Dashboard Improvements

We've also repaired and improved our connections to Amazon Web Services and Salesforce. Our Amazon connection now provides you with the ability to report against your cloud data in EC2, EBS, SQS, and RDS. Meanwhile, our Salesforce connection has been updated to take advantage of OAuth to guarantee the necessary level of security for us to restore that connection to the Connections page.

Refreshing your Basecamp and Highrise data sources should now be much faster than it previously was. In particular, the synchronization time required for Highrise notes should be far faster than it was previously, so if you wanted to report on notes before and just found it taking too long, give it another try!

Bar and Column Chart Improvements

We've made a number of improvements to bar and column charts, in particular enabling you to combine multiple measures in the context of these chart types. This functionality enables you to compare actual vs. estimated sales per channel, actual vs. estimated hours per project, and other such analyses.

Tag Improvements and Fixes

Several of you asked for a way to filter on multiple tags at the same time. You can do that now--just create your normal filters against the tag field, and it'll do the magic behind the scenes to filter down the results to anything containing both "Tag 1" and "Tag 2." You can also now join composites data sources on tag fields.

Highrise Email Fixes

Our previous iteration of reporting on Highrise emails had a number of issues. We've resolved those issues so that you should be able to much more effectively do reporting against email activity in Highrise.

Continued fixes

As always, we've been continuing to make fixes wherever possible. If you encounter any errors or other bugs, please let us know and we'll work to resolve the issues as quickly as possible.

Easy Insight on Twitter

Just a reminder to follow Easy Insight on Twitter at http://twitter.com/easyinsight to keep up to date on the latest changes!

Coming soon...

The documentation overhaul and new wizards mentioned in the last newsletter are still steadily coming along. We have a number of scorecard enhancements such as email delivery, export, and integration into dashboards coming soon. We're in the process of redesigning our approach to the population of lookup tables so that you can update values within the context of the report editor, making it easier to find and update the values you need in large data sets. We have some added functionality in the works to automatically import your users from your connected systems into Easy Insight, making user administration easier and faster. And much, much more!
