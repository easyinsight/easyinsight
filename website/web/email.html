The Last Year

It's been a great year for Easy Insight. After going to market at the beginning of the year, we've seen thousands of new users sign up. Next year should be even better, as we roll out more connections, more features, continue to improve our quality of service, and provide the best SaaS business intelligence offering in the market! And some random goodies to end the year on...

Dashboards

Want to combine multiple reports into a single dashboard? You can do just that now! You'll notice a "Create Dashboard" button on My Data--you can use that to set up a dashboard, using grids to set up layout and stacks to combine multiple reports into tabbed panes. A couple of sample dashboards are available in the Exchange for Basecamp and Highrise users--we'll be working to produce more and to continue refining and improving this functionality over the coming weeks.

Report styling

We've tweaked some of the report look and feel, particularly around charts. If you weren't a huge fan of the ocean column chart, there are more options that look better than ever available to you now. Click on Edit Chart Configuration in the report editor to change the report styling to whatever best fits your aesthetic preferences.

Google Analytics and Docs

Our connections to Google Analytics and Google Spreadsheets have been reworked to use a more robust connection mechanism. You should find these systems more reliable and useful now.

Quickbase

We're still putting the finishing touches on this one, but if you're a user of Quickbase, we have a beta version of a connection enabling you to apply the full reporting and analytic capabilities of Easy Insight to Quickbase data. If you're interested in trying it out, please let us know!

Basecamp Todo Comments

One frequently requested feature has been the ability to report on todo comments in Basecamp. We're still working to fully accomodate this request, but we at least offer the option of reporting on todo comments for Open todo items now. If you go to your Basecamp server configuration in Easy Insight, you'll find this option along with the others such as Include Milestone Comments and Include Archived Projects.

Composite Data Source Fixes

We've made a large number of fixes to composite data sources--the ability to join arbitrary data sources together into a single Easy Insight data source. Want to combine Basecamp, Highrise, a Google spreadsheet, and Freshbooks into a single report? This functionality can help you achieve that goal.

Thanks again to all of our users

A huge thanks to all of our users. It's been a great year, and I'm sure that 2011 will be even better. Happy holidays!
